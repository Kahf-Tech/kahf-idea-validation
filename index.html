Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Idea Validation feature.

How does Idea Validation integrate with my existing CRM data?
When you add target segments, the system automatically scans your existing leads and shows how many match each segment's criteria. You can also manually link specific leads as validation evidence.
What stages can an idea go through?
Every idea progresses through: Concept (just captured) → Researching (gathering data) → Validating (testing assumptions) → Approved (ready to build) or Rejected (killed with reason captured).
Can multiple team members work on the same idea?
Absolutely. You can assign different researchers to different target segments within the same idea. Each person adds their findings, and managers can review everything before making a decision.
Is there a way to export validation reports?
Currently in development. The Professional plan will include PDF export of complete validation reports, including segment research, matched leads, and decision history.
How long does setup take?
Most teams are up and running within 24 hours. No technical expertise needed — if you already use KahfCRM, the Idea Validation tab appears automatically with your existing team permissions.
Can I approve an idea and create a campaign immediately?
Yes! When you approve an idea, you have the option to automatically create an email campaign in KahfCRM's marketing module — perfect for launching new services to your lead database.
