Why a Structured Validation Process?

South Asian consultancies evaluate dozens of new products every year. Most decisions are gut-feel. Change that.

📥

Capture Instantly

Log product ideas before they're forgotten — new destinations, courses, services, or packages.

🎯

Define Target Users

Add segments with demographics, pain points, and estimated market size. Link real leads as evidence.

🔬

Research & Validate

Assign team members to research segments. Add findings, attach files, and track validation status.

📊

Data-Driven Decisions

See matched lead counts, validation progress, and team input — all in one place before green-lighting.

📎

Link Real Leads

Connect leads from your CRM as validation samples. Instantly see how many matches exist per segment.

🔄

Track Progress

Move ideas through stages: Concept → Researching → Validating → Approved/Rejected. Full activity log.
