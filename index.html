Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about KahfCRM.

What makes KahfCRM different from other CRMs?
KahfCRM is purpose-built for South Asian education consultancies. Unlike generic CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), we understand the local workflow — from bKash payments to university-specific application tracking, education models, and WhatsApp-first communication.
Can I migrate my existing data from spreadsheets?
Yes! We support CSV/Excel import for leads, students, and applications. Our onboarding team will help map your fields and get everything set up within 24-48 hours.
Is WhatsApp integration included?
WhatsApp messaging is available in the Professional plan and above. Send messages, track conversation history, and log communication right inside the lead record.
Do you offer local payment methods?
Absolutely. We support bKash, Nagad, and bank transfers for Bangladesh-based consultancies. International payments via credit/debit card are also accepted.
How does the Idea Validation module work?
Capture product ideas (new destinations, courses, services), define target student segments, assign researchers, track validation progress through stages (Concept → Researching → Validating → Approved/Rejected), and link real CRM leads as evidence. Makes go/no-go decisions data-backed.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. You get full access to all features in your chosen plan. If you don't continue, your data is safely deleted.
